LOGO COMPETITION - DEADLINE: APRIL 16, 2025
Your Logo Here

Rules & Contest Details

  • Each participant may submit up to 3 entries
  • All entries will be judged anonymously by Fr. Sylvester
  • Winner will be announced at our arts night and showcase on Saturday, May 17

Logo Requirements

  • Must fit within a circular frame
  • Must include color (not black and white only)
  • Must include the name "Legio Caeli" (Legion of Heaven)
  • Must incorporate a callback or reference to St. Maria Goretti
  • The logo must incorporate green or a related shade, as it serves as the youth group's primary color

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity and originality
  • Visual appeal and aesthetic quality
  • Clear representation of "Legio Caeli" concept
  • Meaningful incorporation of elements or symbolism to St. Maria Goretti
  • Effective use of green or derivatives of green as the primary color
  • Legibility and clarity at different sizes
  • Relevance to Catholic youth ministry

Submission Guidelines

  • Accepted file formats: JPG, PNG, or PDF
  • Minimum resolution: 300 dpi (higher resolutions welcome)
  • Can be created via digital art (e.g., Procreate, Photoshop) or traditional media (paint, ink, etc.) that is scanned or photographed
  • Each entry must be submitted with a brief explanation (max 100 words) describing the symbolism and design choices
  • Email submissions to youth@smgcc.net with subject line "Logo Contest: [Your Name]"
  • Include your name, age, and contact information in the email body, not on the artwork

Additional Information

  • The winning logo will become the official emblem of Legio Caeli Youth Group
  • The winner will receive recognition in the parish bulletin and on social media
  • By submitting, you grant St. Maria Goretti Parish permission to use, modify, and reproduce the logo
  • Designs must be original work and not infringe on any copyrights

Design Suggestions

St. Maria Goretti Symbols:

✿ Lilies (representing purity)
★ Stars ("stella maris" - star of the sea)
🌾 Farming implements (her background)
👑 Halo or crown (symbolizing sainthood)
  • Imagery should convey "Legion of Heaven" in a positive, empowering way
  • Design should work well on t-shirts, social media, and printed materials
  • Consider how the logo will look in both color and grayscale formats
